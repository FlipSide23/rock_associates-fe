About Rock Associates

Rock Associates Company Limited was initially created in 2010, in the Republic of Kenya. Our Company, “Rock Associates Company Ltd” is a Planning Powerful Company, very experienced in the fields of Urban - Regional and Strategic Planning assisted by GIS and Remote Sensing, Policy Formulation, Project management, Green Engineering and Architectural Planning, Land Surveying Activities and Environmental & Social Studies. Rock Associates Company Limited is a multi-disciplined and professional Consultancy Firm, officially registered in the Republic of Kenya since the year of 2010 and in the republic of Rwanda since the year of 2015 endowed with expertise in working with Local Communities, Municipalities, Local Governments, Central Governments, Civil Society Organizations, Development Partners, etc. The firm has worked closely with central governments, local authorities, communities, and professional associations to provide quality and prompt advisory consultancy services. The selected key completed projects and assignments are provided within the Section related to Company Profiling. Ongoing Projects are presented in the same section as well.
